🧶What is Crochet?

My favourite hobby

A needlecraft in which we use a hook to create fabric from loops of yarn.

Crochet! is a versatile craft that can be used to create a wide variety of items. Some of the most popular things to crochet include blankets, hats, scarves, and dishcloths. However, you are not limited to these items! With a little creativity, you can crochet just about anything you can imagine.
